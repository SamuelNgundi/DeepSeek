Our Projects

Githunguri Constituency stands as a beacon of agricultural excellence in Kiambu County, renowned as the highest milk-producing constituency in Kenya. Beyond dairy, our region is a thriving hub for coffee, tea, avocados, and increasingly, banana farming. Under the visionary leadership of Hon. Gathoni Wamuchomba, Member of Parliament for Githunguri, we are transforming this agricultural landscape through innovative banana value addition projects.

Our initiatives are strategically aligned with key UN Sustainable Development Goals, directly contributing to Zero Hunger (SDG 2), Poverty Reduction (SDG 1), Gender Equality (SDG 5), and Climate Action (SDG 13). Through smart banana farming and value addition, we're creating sustainable livelihoods while protecting our environment.

Agriculture Hub of East Africa

Githunguri leads in diverse agricultural production, from dairy to bananas, serving as a model for sustainable farming practices.

Highest Milk Producing Constituency

Renowned for dairy excellence, Githunguri sets the standard for agricultural productivity and farmer organization.

Banana Smart Farming Projects

Pioneering innovative banana cultivation and value addition techniques that maximize farmer income and sustainability.

Our Core Projects

üè¢ Banana Ripening Centers

Our modern banana ripening facilities revolutionize the traditional ripening process, significantly shortening the ripening period while ensuring consistent quality and market readiness.

  • Faster Market Access: Reduces ripening time by 40% compared to traditional methods
  • Increased Farmer Income: Quicker market readiness means higher prices and reduced losses
  • Quality Assurance: Controlled environment ensures consistent, premium-quality bananas
  • SDG Alignment: Direct contribution to UNSDG Agenda 1 (Poverty Reduction)

Result: 60% increase in farmer income through improved market access and reduced post-harvest losses.

üßµ Banana Fiber Extraction & Value Addition

Transforming banana stems from agricultural waste into valuable products, creating new income streams for farmers while promoting sustainable waste management.

Dual Benefits: Farmers earn extra income by selling stems, while the water from stems is converted into organic foliar fertilizer, creating a complete zero-waste cycle.

Creates employment opportunities for women, youth, and differently-abled individuals, promoting UNSDG 5 (Gender Equality) & Climate Action.

Our Methodology

Our systematic approach ensures sustainable success through community engagement, proper training, and market integration.

  1. Distribute banana seedlings to homesteads across Githunguri
  2. Train farmers and form cooperatives/self-help groups
  3. Establish collection & ripening centers for efficient processing
  4. Guarantee markets & fair pricing for all banana produce
  5. Collect stems and process into fiber & organic fertilizer
  6. Develop production units for sanitary and textile products
